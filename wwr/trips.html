Trips

If this is your first time, or if you are looking for a calm trip, we would recommend the beginner's adventure. Perfect for children, families, and nature lovers looking to check out rafting.

If you are eager to jump right into true rafting, this is the trip for you. This trip includes smooth sections to help you get your barings, just before the fun begins and the waters get rough. Still managable for newer rafters, yet exciting enough to those already hooked.

One of our more challenging trips, catered to those looking to work the entire time. Upper body strength is a requirement on this trip, as the twists and turns keep you on your toes.

Our all day trip includes beautiful scenes along the path, with smooth and rough waters throughout. Rafters will have the chance to come onshore for a lunch break, to regain their strenght and courage for the journey back.

This trip requires an extra waiver to be signed before jumping in the raft, and our experienced guides are on high alert to keep everyone in the raft. The adrenaline pumps on this high tides, rapids and falls trip.
