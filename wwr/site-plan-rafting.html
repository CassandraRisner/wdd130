Overview

Purpose

We strive to build a community of rafters at all skill levels, coming together to provide safe and exciting rafting experiences.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Anyone from beginner to expert level rafters looking to connect with others who share their love of rafting. What do they want? To connect with others who share their love of rafting, to plan exciting and safe trips together. What do they expect to find on the website? Information on upcoming gatherings or trips with others in the community.
