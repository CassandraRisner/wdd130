History

A brief history of the company. We are a family owned business with rafters of every level. Our son has been rafting for years, and invited mom and dad along last year. Since that time, we go rafting as a family almost weekly. Mom often stays behind with the car and food, knowing the boys will return when they are hungry. All members of the family are trained and certified instructors and trip guides, willing to help your family find their next adventure together.
